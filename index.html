    About Us

    The Hospital was established by spouses Arturo P. Pingoy, MD and Amparo Y. Pinggoy, MD on May 12, 1961.

    • In 1971, four private air-conditioned rooms were put up, followed by the construction of a two-storey building that housed 8 private rooms and 4 suite rooms in 1974.
    • In 1998, the hospital’s bed capacity was expanded to 50 with the construction of 40 private rooms, financed by the Land Bank of the Philippines.....

    In May 2012, the construction of a new building began, expanding the hospital into a premier facility with a 3-storey building. This new addition will house patient rooms, doctors’ clinics, commercial spaces, and a medical arts area. The hospital has also been recognized by PhilHealth as a Center of Safety and a Center of Quality.

    Client's Common Questions That We Might Have Answers

    01 What services does the hospital provide?

    Our hospital offers a wide range of services, including emergency care, inpatient and outpatient surgery, diagnostic imaging, maternity care, pediatric care, cardiology, orthopedics, and more. For a complete list of services and specialties, please visit our Services page on our website or contact our customer service.

    02 What are the hospital’s visitor policies?

    Our visitor policies vary by department and the patient’s condition. Generally, visitors are allowed between 9:00 AM and 8:00 PM. For specific details or restrictions, please contact the patient’s nursing station or check our website for the latest visitor guidelines.

    03 What are the hospital’s operating hours?

    Our hospital is open 24/7 for emergency services. For non-emergency services, our general operating hours are Monday through Friday from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM. Some departments may have different hours, so please check with the specific department for details.
